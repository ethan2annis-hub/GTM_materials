# TONE & VOICE

Following BILL.com's brand voice with Ethan's authentic, conversational style:

**Voice Principles:**
* **Confidently helpful, not corporate:** Sound like a smart finance leader explaining this to a peer over coffee, not a press release
* **Problem-focused before solution-focused:** Lead with the pain (receipt chasing sucks) before the feature (AI categorization)
* **Concrete, not abstract:** Use specific time savings ("10-15 hours per month") instead of vague claims ("more efficient")
* **Straight-talking, not hyperbolic:** We eliminated receipt capture—that's radical enough without overselling

**Examples of Voice in Action:**

❌ **Corporate/Generic:** "BILL's innovative Touchless Transactions feature leverages advanced AI technology to streamline expense management workflows and enhance operational efficiency."

✅ **BILL Voice:** "The entire industry tried to make receipt capture easier. BILL eliminated it entirely. Your employees spend money, and we do everything else."

❌ **Corporate/Generic:** "Our solution provides significant time savings for finance professionals through automated processes."

✅ **BILL Voice:** "Finance teams spend 10-15 hours per month chasing receipts. Touchless Transactions gives you that time back—automatically."

**Casual Words to Use:** Stoked, obvi, totes, bananas (sparingly—lean professional for B2B finance audience but keep warmth)

**Emojis:** Very sparingly (only in social, not in sales materials)

Every expense management platform in the industry has worked toward the same goal: make receipt capture easier.

Better mobile cameras. Faster OCR. Smarter cropping. Automatic merchant detection.

But here's the thing: **faster data entry is still data entry.**

Finance teams still spend 10-15 hours per month chasing missing receipts. Employees still hate expense reports. Controllers still delay month-end close waiting on documentation from three weeks ago.

We asked a different question: **What if receipt capture didn't exist at all?**

Think about where receipts live:

• Email (Gmail, Outlook)—order confirmations, invoices, delivery receipts
• Apps (Lyft, Uber, DoorDash)—automatic confirmations
• Text messages—SMS receipts from vendors

Employees don't need to capture receipts. **Receipts already captured themselves.**

So instead of making employees photograph receipts or forward emails, BILL Touchless Transactions connects directly to these sources and pulls receipts automatically.

Employee takes an Uber → Receipt appears in BILL instantly → Done.

No app to open. No photo to take. No email to forward.

**Section 3: AI That Does More Than Read Receipts**

Most expense platforms use AI to read receipts after employees upload them. OCR pulls merchant name and amount, maybe suggests a category.

That's helpful—but it's still reactive.

BILL Touchless Transactions uses AI proactively:

• **Categorizes transactions** based on merchant, amount, and your company's existing patterns
• **Generates business notes** contextually (e.g., "Ground transportation" for Uber, "Meals & Entertainment" for client dinners)
• **Learns from corrections** to improve accuracy over time

By the time an employee opens BILL, their expense is already categorized with a business note attached. They review and submit—done in 30 seconds.

**"Employees actually like submitting expenses now."**
The #1 complaint about expense reports—manual receipt capture—is gone.

**Section 5: This Is Just the Start**

Touchless Transactions is available now for BILL Spend & Expense customers.

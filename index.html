What

Overview BILL is eliminating the need for employees to manually capture receipts by automatically pulling receipts from integrated sources and using AI to categorize transactions with business notes—no phone scans, no manual uploads, just done.

Key Features & Functionality

1. Receipt Capture Integrations

Automatic receipt retrieval from Gmail, Lyft, Outlook (upcoming), Uber (upcoming), and text messages—employees never touch their phone to capture a receipt.

2. AI-Powered Categorization

Machine learning model automatically categorizes transactions and creates accurate business notes the moment spending happens—no manual data entry.

3. Zero-Touch Workflow

Employees spend, receipts appear, transactions are categorized, reports are ready—all without lifting a finger.

Top Value Propositions

  • Your employees never touch a receipt again. We're not making it easier—we're making it invisible.
  • AI that actually works. High-accuracy categorization and business notes that CFOs and controllers can trust.
  • Time back to what matters. What used to take 20 minutes of tedious work now happens automatically in seconds.

Limitations

  • Receipt capture integrations require employees to connect their Gmail/Outlook accounts or existing Lyft/Uber integrations
  • AI categorization accuracy depends on transaction data quality and company category rules
  • Text receipt capture requires SMS forwarding setup

Future Additions

  • Additional integrations (Amazon Business, other travel platforms)
  • Custom AI training based on company-specific categorization patterns
  • Receipt matching for invoices and multi-line item purchases
  • Mobile app notifications when AI needs clarification on unusual transactions

Why

Problem Statement: Every expense report starts with the same soul-crushing task: hunt down receipts. Employees dig through email, take photos of crumpled paper receipts, manually type in merchant names and categories. Finance teams chase down missing receipts for weeks. The entire industry has tried to make this easier with phone cameras and OCR—but we're eliminating the problem entirely.

After: Sales rep takes an Uber to client meeting → BILL automatically pulls receipt from Uber integration → AI categorizes as "Ground Transportation" → AI generates business note based on transaction context → expense is ready to submit. Done.

How

For Admins

  1. Enable Touchless Transactions in BILL settings
  2. Set up company categorization rules (optional—AI works out of the box)
  3. Invite employees to connect integrations (Gmail/Outlook, Lyft, Uber)
  4. Turn on text receipt forwarding for employees who want it

For Employees

  1. Connect Gmail or Outlook account in BILL app (one-time, 2 minutes)
  2. Connect Lyft/Uber if used for business travel
  3. Set up text forwarding for SMS receipts (optional)
  4. Spend as usual—receipts and categorization happen automatically

Problem Statement

Expense reports suck because of one thing: receipts. Employees hunt through email, photograph crumpled paper, and manually type merchant names. Finance teams spend hours chasing down missing receipts. Controllers delay month-end close waiting for that one sales rep to submit their Uber receipts from three weeks ago.

The entire industry tried to solve this with phone cameras and OCR. But making receipt capture "easier" still means employees are spending 15-20 minutes per expense report on data entry. Finance teams are still playing receipt detective.

The problem isn't that receipt capture is hard. The problem is that it exists at all.

Old Process

  • Employees: Get receipt via email or paper → Open expense app → Take photo or upload PDF → Manually select category → Type business purpose → Submit → Repeat for every single transaction
  • Finance Teams: Review submitted expenses → Chase down missing receipts → Correct miscategorized transactions → Manually add missing business context → Follow up with employees who forget → Delay month-end close
  • Result: 10-15 hours per month of pure administrative waste. Frustrated employees. Controllers who spend more time on expense cleanup than actual financial analysis.

Solution and Messaging (Customer Facing)

BILL Touchless Transactions makes receipt capture invisible. We automatically retrieve receipts from Gmail, Lyft, Uber, Outlook, and text messages—no manual uploads, no phone cameras, no data entry. Then our AI categorizes every transaction and adds business notes the moment spending happens.

Your employees spend money. We handle the rest.

  • Your team never touches a receipt again. We're not making it easier—we're making it disappear. No photos, no forwarding, no uploads. Just done.
  • AI that actually saves time. High-accuracy auto-categorization and intelligent business notes that finance teams can trust. Not AI for AI's sake—AI that gives you 10-15 hours back every month.
  • Expense reports that fill themselves out. Employees spend. Receipts appear. Categories are assigned. Business notes are written. Reports are ready to submit. All automatic.
  • Month-end close that doesn't wait on receipts. No more chasing down missing receipts. No more follow-up emails. No more delays. Clean data, complete documentation, audit-ready reports.
  • The only truly touchless expense workflow. Everyone else optimized the camera. We deleted it entirely.

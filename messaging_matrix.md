BILL is doing something radically different: we're eliminating receipt capture entirely. With Touchless Transactions, employees never touch a receipt again. We automatically pull receipts from where they already exist (Gmail, Lyft, Uber, text messages) and use AI to categorize transactions with business notes the moment spending happens. **Elevator Pitch:** BILL Touchless Transactions eliminates the most hated part of expense reports—receipt capture. That's 10-15 hours per month back to your finance team.

## Problem Statement

Expense reports suck because of one thing: receipts. Employees hunt through email, photograph crumpled paper, and manually type merchant names. Finance teams spend hours chasing down missing receipts. Controllers delay month-end close waiting for that one sales rep to submit their Uber receipts from three weeks ago.

The entire industry tried to solve this with phone cameras and OCR. But making receipt capture "easier" still means employees are spending 15-20 minutes per expense report on data entry. Finance teams are still playing receipt detective.

The problem isn't that receipt capture is hard. The problem is that it exists at all.

### Old Process

* **Employees:** Get receipt via email or paper → Open expense app → Take photo or upload PDF → Manually select category → Type business purpose → Submit → Repeat for every single transaction
* **Finance Teams:** Review submitted expenses → Chase down missing receipts → Correct miscategorized transactions → Manually add missing business context → Follow up with employees who forget → Delay month-end close
* **Result:** 10-15 hours per month of pure administrative waste. Frustrated employees. Controllers who spend more time on expense cleanup than actual financial analysis.

## Competitive Information

The expense management market has converged around the same solution: make receipt capture easier with mobile apps and OCR. But they all still require manual effort.

**Expensify:** Industry leader in receipt scanning—but employees still take photos of every receipt and manually categorize. Their AI reads receipts but doesn't eliminate the capture step.

**Concur (SAP):** Enterprise-focused, clunky UX. Employees email receipts or use mobile app to photograph. Heavy manual data entry. Slow.

**Ramp & Brex:** Modern spend management platforms with auto-categorization based on merchant codes—but employees still manually forward receipts via email or text. No deep integrations. Business notes are still manual.

**Divvy (BILL):** Auto-categorization for card transactions, but receipt capture is still manual upload or photo.

**BILL Touchless Transactions:** The only platform that combines automatic receipt retrieval from email/apps with AI-powered categorization and business notes. Zero employee effort from transaction to categorized expense. We didn't optimize the camera—we deleted it.

**Key Differentiators:**
* Only solution with deep integrations (Gmail, Outlook, Lyft, Uber, SMS) that pull receipts before employees think about them
* Only AI that handles both receipt capture AND intelligent categorization with business notes in one flow
* Only platform where "touchless" actually means touchless—not "easier photo capture"

## What

| Overview phrase/sentence on what is being changed or added: | BILL is eliminating the need for employees to manually capture receipts by automatically pulling receipts from integrated sources and using AI to categorize transactions with business notes—no phone scans, no manual uploads, just done. |
| :---- | :---- |
| **What key features/functionality and their benefits?** | **1. Receipt Capture Integrations:** Automatic receipt retrieval from Gmail, Lyft, Outlook (upcoming), Uber (upcoming), and text messages—employees never touch their phone to capture a receipt.<br><br>**2. AI-Powered Categorization:** Machine learning model automatically categorizes transactions and creates accurate business notes the moment spending happens—no manual data entry.<br><br>**3. Zero-Touch Workflow:** Employees spend, receipts appear, transactions are categorized, reports are ready—all without lifting a finger. |
| **What are the top value propositions or key messages?** | • **Your employees never touch a receipt again.** We're not making it easier—we're making it invisible.<br>• **AI that actually works.** High-accuracy categorization and business notes that CFOs and controllers can trust.<br>• **Time back to what matters.** What used to take 20 minutes of tedious work now happens automatically in seconds. |
| **Are there any limitations to this new feature?** | • Receipt capture integrations require employees to connect their Gmail/Outlook accounts or existing Lyft/Uber integrations<br>• AI categorization accuracy depends on transaction data quality and company category rules<br>• Text receipt capture requires SMS forwarding setup |
| **Are there any possible future additions?** | • Additional integrations (Amazon Business, other travel platforms)<br>• Custom AI training based on company-specific categorization patterns<br>• Receipt matching for invoices and multi-line item purchases<br>• Mobile app notifications when AI needs clarification on unusual transactions |

## Why

| Problem statement: | Every expense report starts with the same soul-crushing task: hunt down receipts. Employees dig through email, take photos of crumpled paper receipts, manually type in merchant names and categories. Finance teams chase down missing receipts for weeks. The entire industry has tried to make this easier with phone cameras and OCR—but we're eliminating the problem entirely. |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Functional use cases (specific examples of what will change):**  | **Before:** Sales rep takes an Uber to client meeting → gets email receipt → opens BILL app → takes screenshot of email → uploads to expense → manually categorizes as "Ground Transportation" → adds note "Client meeting - Acme Corp" → submits.<br><br>**After:** Sales rep takes an Uber to client meeting → BILL automatically pulls receipt from Uber integration → AI categorizes as "Ground Transportation" → AI generates business note based on transaction context → expense is ready to submit. Done.<br><br>**Before:** Marketing manager orders team lunch via DoorDash → email receipt arrives → saves receipt as PDF → uploads to BILL → types "Meals & Entertainment" → adds note "Team lunch Q4 planning" → manually enters tip amount → submits.<br><br>**After:** Marketing manager orders team lunch → BILL pulls receipt from Gmail integration → AI auto-categorizes → business note auto-populated → receipt attached. Nothing to do.<br><br>**Before:** Controller spends 5 hours per month chasing down missing receipts, following up with employees, and correcting miscategorized expenses.<br><br>**After:** Controller spends 30 minutes per month doing quick spot-checks. Everything else is already done. |
| **How is this different from what our core competitors offer? How does it compare?**  | **Expensify, Concur, Brex:** Still require employees to take photos or forward receipts. Their AI categorizes after you capture—we eliminate the capture step entirely.<br><br>**Ramp, Divvy:** Auto-categorization based on merchant codes, but employees still manually forward receipts and write business notes.<br><br>**BILL:** We're the only platform that combines deep integrations (Gmail, Lyft, Uber, text) with AI that handles both receipt capture AND intelligent categorization with business notes. Zero employee effort. |
| **Advantages of our solution /why we're better value prop:**  | • **The industry's only true touchless receipt flow:** Everyone else optimized the camera experience. We deleted the camera entirely.<br>• **Integration-first approach:** We pull from where receipts already live (email, apps) instead of making employees become data entry clerks.<br>• **AI that does the thinking:** Other platforms use AI to read receipts. Ours reads context, categorizes intelligently, and writes the business notes your finance team actually needs.<br>• **Built for the finance team, not just employees:** Controllers and CFOs get cleaner data, faster month-end close, and actual audit trails without chasing people down. |

Spend as usual—receipts and categorization happen automatically |
